Rules & regulation

GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • Central Library is NO-MOBILE ZONE.
  • Students must wear ID card at all times.
  • Maintain strict silence in the Reading Room and other areas.
  • DO NOT enter the reading or lending section with your baggage.
  • DO NOT crowd around any counter, but stand in queue and wait for your turn.
  • ID card is NON-TRANSFERRABLE. Using someone else’s ID is a punishable offence.
  • Kindly cooperate and interact gently with library staff.
  • In case of any difficulty, library users can approach the Librarian.
  • Only present students and staff of the College can use the Library. No ex-student or outsiders are allowed to use the Library.
  • Any indiscipline in the Library will lead to cancellation of membership and/or serious disciplinary actions.
  • Students and Staff members must settle all library dues to obtain Clearance Certificate from the Library Office on completion of studies or duty.
  • Sleeping, eating and having refreshments inside the Library are strictly prohibited.
  • Do take care of library books and journals.

GUIDELINES FOR SELF-CIRCULATION KIOSK

  • Use only your own valid ID card. Using someone else’s ID card to issue/reissue books is a punishable offence
  • Use only to issue/reissue books, not to return.
  • Two books can be borrowed at a time against your ID
  • Get the due date stamped on the date slip inside the book from the issue counter.

GUIDELINES FOR LENDING SECTION

  • A student can borrow two books at a time for fifteen days, which are further renewable for fifteen days (except in special cases). Books borrowed should not be identical.
  • Be present in person with your own ID card while borrowing/returning the library books.
  • Borrowed books should be returned on or before the stipulated date mentioned on the date-slip inside the book.
  • For delay in returning a book students have to pay a fine of Rs. 2 (Rupees two only) per day per book.
  • Any late return of books accumulates a fine of Rs. 2 per day. When the late fines cross rupees fifty, one has to collect the fine slip from the Library office and deposit the amount at the SIB/College Office ONLY. After paying the fine, submit your student copy of the fine slip to the Library Office for updating fine status.
  • Check the condition of the book at the time of borrowing. If any mutilation is found, report instantly to the concerned library staff. Otherwise, the borrower will be responsible for the damage.
  • Open Access is for your convenience. DO NOT misplace the books while selecting from the shelves. After consulting books DO NOT shuffle them but place them in the PROPER PLACE.
  • Remember, book hiding and misplacing are punishable offences.
  • Return the borrowed books first at the Lending Counter. Don’t go inside the Lending/Reading Section with borrowed books.
  • If books are lost or damaged (including marking on the pages), the borrower must replace the book with the same edition or the latest edition.

GUIDELINES FOR READING SECTION

  • The books marked as “REFERENCE” and periodicals (both journals and magazines) are meant only for reading consultation inside the READING ROOM ONLY and cannot be taken out.
  • You will be allowed to take a maximum of THREE BOOKS at a time for study in the Reading Room.
  • Do not scatter the pages of newspapers when reading.
  • DO NOT misplace books while selecting from the shelves. After consulting books, return them at the counter.
  • Laptops and palmtops are allowed in the library.
  • Books newly acquired are displayed in the New Arrival Section.
