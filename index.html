Welcome to MMHTTC

Maulana Mazharul Haque Teachers' Training College, located in Samastipur, Bihar, was established with the vision of becoming a pioneer in the field of teacher education. The college is not just an institution for academic learning but a center for nurturing values, discipline, and a deep sense of responsibility among future educators.

Run by the Maulana Mazharul Haque Welfare Society, the college aims to integrate modern educational practices with rich cultural and ethical traditions. Our mission is to prepare highly skilled, competent, and compassionate teachers who can meet the challenges of the ever-evolving educational landscape.

We believe that teaching is not just a profession, but a commitment to shaping young minds and building the future of the nation. To fulfill this mission, we provide a supportive and intellectually stimulating environment where students are encouraged to think critically, act ethically, and contribute meaningfully to society.

The college emphasizes innovation, research, and practical training to ensure that our graduates are well-equipped to handle diverse classroom situations. Through a combination of rigorous coursework, hands-on teaching practice, and continuous mentorship, we strive to develop educators who are not only academically strong but also emotionally intelligent and socially aware.

At Maulana Mazharul Haque Teachers' Training College, we are dedicated to fostering a learning community that values academic integrity, inclusion, creativity, and lifelong learning.
