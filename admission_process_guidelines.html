ADMISSION

Admission Procedure

  • Academic Programme: To achieve the desired aims & objectives, the following course has been started in the college — B.Ed. (Bachelor of Education) - 2 Years.

Eligibility

  • Age for B.Ed course: No age limitation
  • Candidates with at least 50% marks either in a Bachelor's Degree and/or in Master's Degree or any equivalent qualification with at least two school subjects at graduation level are eligible.
  • Reservation and relaxation in marks for reserved categories will follow state government rules. 50% of intake seats are reserved for students of the Muslim Community.

Admission Procedure

  • Admission will be made through Combined Entrance Test (CET-B.Ed.) conducted by Bihar State Nodal Agency.
  • All eligibility criteria will follow rules established by the State Nodal Agency.

Admission in D.El.Ed. (Face to Face) - 2 Years

  • Eligibility
  • a. Minimum age limit: 17 years on 1st January of the academic year.
  • b. Minimum 50% marks in Intermediate or 10+2.
  • c. Must have a clear result in Intermediate or 10+2 on 1st January of the academic year of application.

Selection for Admission

  • Eligibility
  • a. Selection will be based on entrance exam, interview/counseling & academic merit. Only complete applications will be considered.
  • b. Entrance exam will include 100 objective questions (General Knowledge, Literature, Science/Arts).
  • c. Exam will be conducted at the college campus.

Fee Structure

  • Candidates admitted to the 2-year B.Ed. course must pay ₹75,000 per year as per Bihar Government norms.

Note

  • Fees should be paid either in cash or by DD drawn in favor of the Secretary, Maulana Mazharul Haque Teacher's Training College, payable at Samastipur.
